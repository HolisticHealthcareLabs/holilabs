## 5. CONSENT FRAMEWORK

### 5.1 Granular Consent Model

In accordance with the LGPD's requirement for specific and highlighted consent for sensitive data, Holi Labs implements a granular consent model with separate, independent consent toggles:

**Toggle 1 — Service Provision (Required)**
Consent for the processing of personal data and health data necessary for the provision of clinical decision support services through the Platform. This consent is required to use the Platform.

**Toggle 2 — Anonymized Research and Analytics (Optional)**
Consent for the use of anonymized and aggregated data derived from clinical interactions for purposes of scientific research, statistical analysis, and improvement of healthcare services. Data used under this consent toggle is irreversibly de-identified before processing. Default: **OFF**.

**Toggle 3 — Marketing and Communications (Optional)**
Consent for the receipt of commercial communications, newsletters, product announcements, and partner offers from Holi Labs. Default: **OFF**.

### 5.2 Consent Characteristics

All consent obtained by Holi Labs shall be:

- **Free** (*livre*): Not conditioned on the provision of unrelated services.
- **Informed** (*informada*): Accompanied by clear, complete information about the purpose of processing.
- **Unambiguous** (*inequívoca*): Manifested through a clear affirmative action.
- **Specific** (*específica*): Granted for defined, explicit, and legitimate purposes.
- **Highlighted** (*destacada*): For sensitive data, presented separately and prominently from other terms.

### 5.3 Consent Withdrawal

You may withdraw your consent at any time, without cost, through: (a) the consent management interface within the Platform; (b) email to dpo@holilabs.com; or (c) written request to the address specified in Section 14. Withdrawal of consent does not affect the lawfulness of processing carried out prior to withdrawal. Withdrawal of the Service Provision consent (Toggle 1) will result in the inability to use the Platform.

## 9. DATA SUBJECT RIGHTS

### 9.1 Your Rights Under the LGPD (Art. 18)

In accordance with Article 18 of the LGPD, you have the following rights regarding your personal data:

1. **Confirmation of Processing** — The right to obtain confirmation of the existence of processing activities involving your personal data.

2. **Access** — The right to access your personal data processed by Holi Labs.

3. **Correction** — The right to request the correction of incomplete, inaccurate, or outdated personal data.

4. **Anonymization, Blocking, or Deletion** — The right to request the anonymization, blocking, or deletion of unnecessary or excessive data, or data processed in violation of the LGPD.

5. **Portability** — The right to request the portability of your personal data to another service provider, subject to ANPD regulations.
   - *For Patient health data:* Portability requests should be directed to the Healthcare Provider or clinic (Controller), as Holi Labs acts as Processor.
   - *For User account data:* Portability requests may be directed to Holi Labs.

6. **Deletion of Consent-Based Data** — The right to request the deletion of personal data processed on the basis of consent, except where retention is required by law (e.g., medical record retention obligations).

7. **Information About Sharing** — The right to obtain information about the public and private entities with which Holi Labs has shared your data.

8. **Information About Consent** — The right to be informed about the possibility of not providing consent and the consequences of refusal.

9. **Consent Withdrawal** — The right to withdraw consent at any time, through a simple and free process.

10. **Review of Automated Decisions** — The right to request a review of decisions made solely on the basis of automated processing of personal data that affect your interests, including decisions related to profiling, in accordance with Article 20 of the LGPD.

11. **Opposition** — The right to oppose the processing of your personal data when it is carried out in violation of the LGPD.

### 9.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

You may exercise your data subject rights by contacting:

- **Email:** dpo@holilabs.com
- **In-Platform:** Through the data subject rights request feature in the Platform settings.

Holi Labs shall respond to data subject rights requests within fifteen (15) days of receipt, or within such other period as required by ANPD regulation. Requests are processed free of charge.

### 9.3 Limitations on Erasure

The right to erasure (deletion) is subject to the following exceptions, in accordance with Article 16 of the LGPD:

- **Compliance with a legal or regulatory obligation** — Medical records must be retained for twenty (20) years.
- **Research** — Data may be retained for research purposes, provided it is anonymized.
- **Transfer to third parties** — Provided data protection requirements are met.
- **Exclusive use by the Controller** — Provided data is anonymized and does not reach third parties.

### 9.4 Right to Erasure — Process

When a valid erasure request is received and no retention exception applies:

1. All personally identifiable information shall be scrubbed from active databases.
2. Associated vector database embeddings and AI model references shall be re-indexed to remove the data subject's records.
3. A deletion event shall be logged (metadata only — zero personally identifiable information in the log entry).
4. A deletion receipt shall be provided to the data subject, containing: timestamp, scope of deletion, and legal basis (LGPD Art. 18, VI).

## 10. ARTIFICIAL INTELLIGENCE AND AUTOMATED PROCESSING

### 10.1 AI Technologies Used

The Platform utilizes artificial intelligence technologies, including but not limited to:

- **Ambient Clinical Documentation:** Audio capture and natural language processing to generate clinical notes from provider-patient encounters.
- **Clinical Note Generation:** Large language models to produce structured clinical documentation (SOAP notes, discharge summaries, referral letters).
- **Clinical Decision Support Insights:** Pattern analysis and knowledge base queries to surface relevant clinical information for the Healthcare Provider's consideration.
- **Coding Assistance:** Suggested procedure and diagnosis codes (ICD-10, CPT, TUSS) based on clinical documentation.
- **Workflow Optimization:** Intelligent scheduling, task prioritization, and resource allocation suggestions.

### 10.2 AI Is Not a Medical Device

The AI Features are clinical decision support tools that assist — but do not replace — the independent clinical judgment of qualified healthcare professionals. The AI Features do not autonomously perform diagnosis, detection, prevention, or treatment of diseases or health conditions. The Platform does not constitute Software as a Medical Device (SaMD) as defined under ANVISA RDC nº 657/2022.

### 10.3 Human Oversight

All AI-generated Outputs require review, verification, and approval by a qualified Healthcare Provider before clinical application. The Healthcare Provider retains sole, exclusive, and non-delegable responsibility for all clinical decisions, irrespective of whether AI Features were used in connection with such decisions. In accordance with Resolução CFM nº 2.314/2022 and the CFM's March 2026 resolution on AI in medicine, the final clinical decision always remains with the physician, who possesses full autonomy to accept, modify, or reject any AI-generated suggestion.

### 10.4 AI Data Processing Safeguards

- **Anonymization:** Patient data transmitted to third-party AI service providers is anonymized through our anonymization proxy before transmission.
- **Zero Retention:** Third-party AI service providers are contractually prohibited from retaining, storing, or using Patient data beyond the immediate processing session, pursuant to zero-retention Data Processing Agreements.
- **No Training on Identifiable Data:** Individually identifiable Patient data is never used for training, fine-tuning, or improving third-party AI models without the data subject's specific, highlighted consent.
- **Audit Trail:** All AI processing events are logged in an immutable, hash-chain-verified audit trail.

### 10.5 Right to Review Automated Decisions

In accordance with Article 20 of the LGPD, you have the right to request a review of decisions made solely on the basis of automated processing of personal data that affect your interests. You may exercise this right by contacting dpo@holilabs.com. The Healthcare Provider possesses full autonomy to accept, modify, or reject any suggestion presented by the Platform's AI Features.

## 11. DATA SECURITY

### 11.1 Security Measures

Holi Labs implements and maintains a comprehensive information security program designed to protect personal data against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Our security measures include, but are not limited to:

- **Encryption:** All personal data is encrypted at rest and in transit using industry-standard encryption protocols (AES-256 for data at rest; TLS 1.2+ for data in transit).
- **Access Controls:** Role-based access control (RBAC) with principle of least privilege. Multi-factor authentication (MFA) required for all user accounts.
- **Audit Logging:** Comprehensive, immutable, hash-chain-verified audit logs recording all access to and modifications of personal data.
- **Tenant Isolation:** Logical separation of Customer data in multi-tenant architecture, ensuring no cross-tenant data access.
- **Vulnerability Management:** Regular security assessments, penetration testing, and dependency scanning.
- **Employee Training:** Mandatory security and privacy training for all Holi Labs personnel with access to personal data.
- **Incident Response:** Documented incident response plan with defined escalation procedures and notification timelines.

### 11.2 Certifications and Standards

Holi Labs maintains compliance with the following security standards and frameworks:

- SOC 2 Type II (or equivalent, upon certification completion)
- ISO 27018 (cloud privacy controls — via AWS infrastructure)
- OWASP Top Ten (web application security)
- Where applicable, HITRUST CSF
