## PURPOSE OF THIS FORM

This form allows you to control what types of marketing and promotional communications you receive from HoliLabs. This form is separate from communications necessary for your treatment and care, which you will continue to receive regardless of your marketing preferences.

## IMPORTANT: THIS IS OPTIONAL

**You do not need to consent to receive marketing communications to receive healthcare services from HoliLabs.** Your healthcare will not be affected by your decision to opt in or opt out of marketing communications.

## TYPES OF COMMUNICATIONS

### Healthcare Communications (You Cannot Opt Out)

These are essential communications about your healthcare that you will receive regardless of your marketing preferences:

- **Appointment Reminders:** Notifications about upcoming appointments
- **Treatment Follow-Ups:** Reminders about medication refills, test results, or follow-up care
- **Preventive Care Reminders:** Notifications about vaccinations, screenings, and wellness checks
- **Health Alerts:** Critical health and safety information
- **Billing and Insurance:** Information about payments, insurance claims, and financial matters
- **System Notifications:** Updates about your account, security alerts, and service changes
- **Legal Notices:** Required notifications about privacy practices, terms of service, etc.

### Marketing Communications (You Can Choose)

These are promotional and informational communications that you can opt in or out of:

**Health and Wellness Content:**
- Health tips and educational articles
- Wellness program information
- Nutrition and fitness advice
- Disease prevention information
- Seasonal health topics (flu prevention, sun safety, etc.)

**Service Promotions:**
- New services and features
- Special offers and discounts
- Promotional pricing
- Referral programs
- Loyalty rewards

**General News and Updates:**
- Newsletter with health news and practice updates
- Success stories and patient testimonials
- Community health events
- Webinars and educational events
- Research participation opportunities

**Third-Party Offers:**
- Offers from our partners (with appropriate safeguards)
- Product recommendations from trusted vendors
- Discount programs from affiliated organizations

## HIPAA REQUIREMENTS FOR MARKETING

Under HIPAA, we need your authorization to:
- Send you marketing communications that promote products or services
- Share your information with third parties for their marketing purposes
- Receive payment from third parties for marketing communications

**We will NEVER:**
- Sell your Protected Health Information (PHI) without your authorization
- Use your PHI for marketing without your authorization
- Share your PHI with third parties for marketing without your authorization

**De-Identified Information:**
We may use de-identified, aggregated data for marketing purposes without your authorization. This data cannot be traced back to you individually.
