## PURPOSE OF THIS CONSENT

This form explains how we will use electronic health records (EHR) to maintain your health information and asks for your consent to participate in our EHR system.

## WHAT ARE ELECTRONIC HEALTH RECORDS?

Electronic Health Records (EHR) are digital versions of your medical records. Instead of paper charts, your health information is stored electronically in a secure computer system. Your EHR may include:

- Personal and demographic information
- Medical history and current conditions
- Medications and allergies
- Immunization records
- Laboratory test results and reports
- Radiology images and reports
- Progress notes from healthcare providers
- Treatment plans and outcomes
- Billing and insurance information

## BENEFITS OF ELECTRONIC HEALTH RECORDS

- **Improved Care Coordination:** Multiple healthcare providers can access your records when you need care
- **Better Safety:** Alerts for drug interactions, allergies, and potential medical issues
- **Faster Access:** Your information is available immediately when needed
- **Better Communication:** Secure electronic messaging with your healthcare team
- **Patient Portal Access:** You can view your own records, test results, and medical information online
- **Reduced Errors:** Legible records reduce medication and treatment errors
- **Continuity of Care:** Your complete medical history is available even if you change providers

## HOW YOUR INFORMATION WILL BE USED

We will use your electronic health records for:

**Treatment:**
- Providing medical care and treatment
- Coordinating care with other healthcare providers
- Consulting with specialists
- Managing medications and prescriptions
- Ordering and reviewing tests and procedures

**Payment:**
- Billing for services
- Processing insurance claims
- Verifying insurance coverage
- Collecting payment

**Healthcare Operations:**
- Quality improvement
- Staff training and education
- Compliance with regulations
- Business planning and management

## WHO CAN ACCESS YOUR RECORDS

Access to your EHR is strictly controlled:

**Authorized Personnel:**
- Your treating healthcare providers
- Healthcare staff involved in your care
- Billing and administrative staff (limited access)
- Quality improvement staff (for approved projects)

**With Your Authorization:**
- Family members or caregivers you designate
- Other healthcare providers you see
- Your health insurance company
- Other individuals or organizations you authorize

**Access Controls:**
- All users have unique login credentials
- Access is logged and monitored
- Staff can only access records necessary for their job
- Unauthorized access is prohibited and may result in disciplinary action

## SECURITY AND PRIVACY PROTECTIONS

We protect your electronic health records with:

**Technical Safeguards:**
- Encryption of data in transit and at rest
- Firewalls and intrusion detection systems
- Automatic session timeouts
- Regular security updates and patches
- Secure data backup and recovery

**Administrative Safeguards:**
- HIPAA privacy and security policies
- Staff training on privacy and security
- Access controls and authorization procedures
- Incident response procedures
- Regular risk assessments

**Physical Safeguards:**
- Secure data centers with restricted access
- Workstation security controls
- Facility access controls

## YOUR RIGHTS

You have the right to:

- **Access:** View and obtain copies of your electronic health records
- **Amendment:** Request corrections to your records if you believe they are inaccurate
- **Accounting:** Receive a list of disclosures of your information
- **Restrictions:** Request limitations on how we use or share your information
- **Confidential Communications:** Request communication by alternative means or locations
- **Paper Records:** Receive paper copies of your records upon request
- **Revoke Consent:** Withdraw your consent to use EHR (with limitations)

## PATIENT PORTAL ACCESS

By participating in our EHR system, you may be granted access to a patient portal where you can:

- View your medical records and test results
- Schedule appointments
- Request prescription refills
- Send secure messages to your healthcare team
- Update your contact and insurance information
- Access health education materials

**Portal Security:**
You are responsible for:
- Keeping your portal login credentials confidential
- Not sharing your account with others
- Logging out after each session
- Using secure internet connections
- Notifying us immediately if you suspect unauthorized access

## LIMITATIONS AND RISKS

While we take extensive measures to protect your information, you should be aware of potential risks:

- **Technical Failures:** System outages or technical issues may temporarily limit access to your records
- **Cyberattacks:** Despite our security measures, determined attackers may attempt to breach our systems
- **Human Error:** Staff may inadvertently access or disclose information inappropriately
- **Legal Requests:** We may be required by law to disclose your information to courts, law enforcement, or government agencies

**Mitigation:** We continuously monitor our systems, train staff, and implement best practices to minimize these risks.

## EMERGENCY ACCESS

In emergency situations:
- Emergency personnel may access your records to provide life-saving treatment
- We may share your information with emergency responders
- Access without your authorization is permitted when necessary to treat you
