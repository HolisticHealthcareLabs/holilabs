## PURPOSE OF THIS CONSENT

This form allows you to authorize HoliLabs to share your Protected Health Information (PHI) with specific individuals or organizations beyond what is already permitted under HIPAA for treatment, payment, and healthcare operations.

## WHAT IS PROTECTED HEALTH INFORMATION (PHI)?

PHI includes any information about you that:
- Relates to your past, present, or future physical or mental health
- Relates to healthcare services provided to you
- Relates to payment for healthcare services
- Identifies you or could be used to identify you

Examples of PHI include:
- Medical diagnoses and conditions
- Treatment plans and medications
- Laboratory and test results
- Medical images (X-rays, MRIs, etc.)
- Doctor's notes and clinical documentation
- Billing and insurance information
- Demographic information (name, address, date of birth, etc.)

**Revoke Authorization:** You may revoke this authorization at any time by sending written notice to:
- Email: privacy@holilabs.com
- Mail: [Full Address]
- In person at our office

**Important:** Revocation is not effective for:
- Information already disclosed based on this authorization
- Actions already taken in reliance on this authorization
- Disclosures required by law

**Receive a Copy:** You have the right to receive a copy of this signed authorization.

**Inspection:** You have the right to inspect or receive a copy of the information to be disclosed (subject to certain exceptions).

## IMPORTANT INFORMATION ABOUT RE-DISCLOSURE

**Once your information is disclosed to the recipient(s) listed above, it may no longer be protected by federal privacy laws (HIPAA). The recipient may re-disclose your information, and it may no longer be protected.**

Exceptions:
- Recipients who are also covered by HIPAA (healthcare providers, health plans) remain bound by HIPAA requirements
- Substance abuse treatment records remain protected by 42 C.F.R. Part 2
- Recipients may be prohibited from further disclosure by state law

We cannot guarantee the confidentiality of information once it leaves our control.

## FEES FOR COPIES

We may charge a reasonable, cost-based fee for copying and transmitting your records:
- Per page charges for paper copies
- Media costs for electronic copies
- Postage or transmission costs

We will inform you of costs before processing your request. You may choose to reduce the scope of your request to reduce costs.
