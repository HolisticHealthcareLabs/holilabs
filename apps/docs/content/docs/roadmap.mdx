From clinical safety wedge to LATAM health data infrastructure.

## H1 2026 — Clinical Safety Foundation

Cortex Clinic launch. Inpatient cardiology wedge (DOAC safety + discharge follow-up). Bolivia pilot. 50 practitioners. $5K MRR. Johns Hopkins research partnership active.

## H2 2026 — Prevention + Brazil Expansion

Prevention Hub with 7 health domains. Brazil Wave 2. RNDS integration certified. 200 practitioners. $19.9K MRR. First enterprise pilot.

## H1 2027 — Governance + Argentina

Full Governance Console with fleet management. Argentina Wave 3. 600 practitioners. $55K MRR. Enterprise contracts signed.

## H2 2027+ — Data Sovereignty Layer

Patient-controlled data marketplace. 1,500 practitioners. $107.5K MRR ($1.29M ARR). Self-sustaining unit economics.

## 2031 Vision

By 2031, Cortex aims to be the clinical safety standard for Latin American healthcare—processing millions of clinical decisions annually with full auditability. A federated, patient-consented health data layer that makes LATAM's 640 million people active participants in the next generation of medical research—on their own terms.
