## What is Cortex?

Cortex is a **deterministic clinical safety protocol-layer** that sits between the clinician and the patient record. It validates, documents, and governs every clinical decision—from drug-interaction checks to longitudinal care management to billing risk scoring.

We don't replace clinical judgment. We make it faster, safer, and provably compliant.

> **Design principle:** AI handles documentation (low-risk hallucinations acceptable). Deterministic rules handle safety-critical decisions (prescribing, dosing, compliance). The clinician always decides.

## Three Engines

**Safety Engine** — Real-time drug interactions, renal dose adjustments, formulary compliance, billing risk scoring.

**Prevention Hub** — 7 health domains. 50+ evidence-based protocols. AI-powered risk scoring with automated reminders.

**Governance Console** — Live validation stream. Trust scoring. Tamper-evident audit trails. Fleet management.

## Why Cortex?

LATAM healthcare operates in a unique regulatory and cultural context. Clinicians coordinate care over WhatsApp. LGPD creates strict data sovereignty requirements. ANVISA's CDS framework opens a regulatory pathway US-centric solutions can't access. Cortex was built for this reality from the first commit.
