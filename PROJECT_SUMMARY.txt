KEY INSIGHTS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

PROBLEM:
  Supply chain gaps → Diagnostic delays → Poor outcomes (readmissions, 
  escalation) + Health inequity (remote/vulnerable populations get care last)

SOLUTION:
  Multi-objective optimization balancing CLINICAL + EQUITY + COST
  (not just cost reduction)

THREE POWERFUL LEVERS (to test in Mirofish):
  1. Transport Frequency — More frequent routes to remote areas
     Expected impact: HIGH equity, MODERATE cost
  2. Instrument Sharing — Formalize inter-facility borrowing
     Expected impact: MODERATE equity, LOW cost
  3. Buffer Stock — Extra safety stock at high-need facilities
     Expected impact: MODERATE equity, MODERATE cost

EXPECTED OUTCOME:
  Diagnostic completeness ≥95% across all facilities
  Equity score ≥95% for vulnerable populations
  Cost per case ≤ +10% vs. baseline (acceptable trade-off)

FAQ: COMMON CONCERNS & ANSWERS
─────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────────

Q: "How much will this cost?"
A: Mirofish will tell you the exact cost of each scenario. Example:
   +1 transport route/week to underserved area: ~$5K/month
   Extra buffer stock at 3 facilities: ~$10K one-time + $2K/month
   Instrument sharing: $0 (governance only)
   CONTEXT: $5K/month in supply investment prevents ~2-3 readmissions/month 
   ($15K each) = $25K in savings. Net positive.

Q: "Aren't we just moving cost around, not reducing it?"
A: Not if optimized for outcomes. Readmissions are >$15K each.
   Preventable escalations are >$10K each.
   Extra supply investment if it prevents those costs is a bargain.

Q: "How do we know the model is accurate?"
A: We validate against baseline (actual 2025 data). If model predicts
   what actually happened, we trust projections. If not, we adjust.

Q: "What if we don't have 12 months of data?"
A: 6 months + clinical judgment works. We'll adjust seasonal factors.
   First year will be a learning year; refine in Year 2.

Q: "Is instrument sharing too complicated?"
A: Protocol is simple (SMS request + hub delivers + spoke returns).
   Pilot at 2-3 facilities first. Build business case before scaling.

Q: "Will this work in a resource-limited setting?"
A: Yes. Democratization is about optimizing what you have, not buying more.
   Sharing, better transport planning, and targeted buffer stock are
   relatively low-cost levers.
