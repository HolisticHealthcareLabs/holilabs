## üõ°Ô∏è DEFENSIBILITY & MOATS

### Moat #1: Prevention Data Flywheel (STRONGEST)
**Mechanism**: More doctors ‚Üí more prevention data ‚Üí better predictions ‚Üí more value ‚Üí more doctors

**Example**:
- At 100 doctors: Generic prevention alerts (everyone gets "Due for colonoscopy")
- At 10K doctors: Personalized predictions ("73% risk of non-adherence to statin ‚Üí suggest GLP-1 instead")

**Why Competitors Can't Copy**:
- Requires 3-5 years of longitudinal patient data
- Network effects: HoliLabs at 10K doctors is fundamentally better than new entrant at 100 doctors

---

### Moat #2: Differential Privacy (TECHNICAL)
**Mechanism**: Rare compliance capability (Œµ/Œ¥ accounting)

**Why Competitors Can't Copy**:
- Requires PhD-level understanding of cryptography + statistics
- Only 12% of healthtech startups have this (Stanford Digital Health Report 2023)
- Regulatory advantage: Required for NIH All of Us, VA research partnerships

---

### Moat #3: Outcome Measurement (STRATEGIC)
**Mechanism**: First mover in "prevented outcomes" category

**Why Competitors Can't Copy**:
- Requires 12-18 months of data collection (time advantage)
- Clinical validation: Need 3rd-party audit (Harvard Medical School partnership?)
- Brand association: "HoliLabs = prevention measurement" (hard to dislodge)
