## 5. Pricing Psychology & Strategy

### Anchoring Strategy

**Price Presentation**:
1. Show **annual pricing** first (makes monthly seem cheap)
2. Highlight **savings** (e.g., "Save $50 with annual billing")
3. Compare to **competitor unbundled pricing** ($120-270/month)
4. Emphasize **time saved** (3-4 hours/day = $150-200 value)

### Freemium ‚Üí Premium Conversion Tactics

**First 100 Program** (Current):
- Free for 1 year ‚Üí Professional tier ($750 value)
- After year 1: Offer 50% discount for year 2 ($375)
- Lock in early adopters with loyalty pricing

**Free Trial Strategy**:
- 30-day free trial of Professional tier (not Starter)
- Show full platform value
- Conversion rate target: 25-35%

**Friend & Family Codes**:
- 1 year free ‚Üí Professional tier
- Viral growth loop
- Target: 200 signups in first 6 months

### Upsell Paths

**Starter ‚Üí Professional**:
- Trigger at 50 patients: "You've reached your limit. Upgrade for unlimited!"
- Trigger at 10 appointments/day
- Offer when user tries restricted features (prevention hub, CDS)
- **Target conversion**: 40% within 6 months

**Professional ‚Üí Enterprise**:
- Trigger at 10+ practitioners
- Offer when multi-site needs arise
- Custom demo with sales team
- **Target conversion**: 20% of Professional customers by year 2
